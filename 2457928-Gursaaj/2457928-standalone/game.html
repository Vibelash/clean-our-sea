How to play

  • Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to steer the snake.
  • Each piece of plastic trash is worth +10 points.
  • Every 50 points a random effect kicks in for a few seconds — Double Points, Calm Waters, Fast Tide, Trash Storm, or Ghost Mode.
  • Don't crash into yourself — that ends the round (unless Ghost Mode is active).
  • Press Space to pause and R to reset.
  • When you lose, your score is saved to the community leaderboard.

The problem

  • 8M tonnes of plastic enter the ocean every year. UN Environment Programme
  • 800+ species of marine life are affected by ocean debris. UNESCO-IOC
  • 5.25 trillion plastic pieces already float in our oceans. PLOS ONE, 2014
  • 450 years is how long a single plastic bottle can take to decompose. NOAA
  • 1 in 3 fish caught for human consumption contains plastic. University of Plymouth

What this game is doing about it

Snake Infinity isn't cleaning real plastic — but it turns the abstract crisis into something you can feel. Every round you play, every piece of trash your snake collects, is tracked by our backend and added to a shared community total. The goal is simple: make the scale of the problem tangible, and make the act of "cleaning" feel collective.
