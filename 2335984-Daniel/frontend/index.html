Clean Our Sea

Our project, "Clean Our Sea", is a digital platform designed to combat marine pollution through community coordination and environmental education. The idea originated from observing how coastal pollution continues to harm marine biodiversity and local livelihoods, yet existing cleanup efforts are often isolated, untracked, or lack of real-time coordination.

The app enables users to report pollution sites, organize or join beach cleanups and track their environmental impact. To increase engagement, it includes gamified features such as leaderboards, achievements, and weekly goals. Users can also access educational games and courses about waste management and sustainability to reinforce positive behavior change.
