Clean Our Sea

Our project, "Clean Our Sea", is a digital platform designed to combat marine pollution through community co-ordination and environmental education. The idea originated from observing how coastal pollution continues to harm marine biodiversity and local livelihoods, yet existing cleanup efforts are often isolated, untracked, or lack real-time co-ordination.

The app enables users to report pollution sites, organise or join beach cleanups and track their environmental impact. To increase engagement, it includes gamified features such as leaderboards, achievements, and weekly goals. Users can also access educational games and courses about waste management and sustainability to reinforce positive behaviour change.
